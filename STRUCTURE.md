# Repository Structure — Legal

This document describes the structure of the Legal repository.

## Root

- `README.md` — overview of the Legal repository  
- `STATUS.md` — current role and state  
- `VERSION` — repository‑level version marker  
- `CHANGELOG.md` — history of repository‑level changes  
- `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` — behaviour expectations  
- `CONTRIBUTING.md` — contribution process  
- `GOVERNANCE.md` — repository governance model  
- `LICENSE` — licensing and legal framing  

## Legal Surfaces

- `privacy-policy.md` — privacy rules for all SCU public surfaces  
- `terms-of-use.md` — legal terms for interacting with SCU services  
- `licensing.md` — licensing rules for SCU content and code  
- `attribution.md` — attribution requirements  
- `safety-disclosure.md` — safety‑related legal disclosures  
- `security-disclosure.md` — security‑related legal disclosures  

These documents define the legal framing for:

- REPO: Governance  
- REPO: Standards  
- REPO: Specifications  
- REPO: Architecture  
- All public surfaces  
- All SCU Foundation programs
