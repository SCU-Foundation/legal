# Privacy Policy  
**SCU Foundation — Legal & Compliance**  
**Status:** Canonical (Pre‑Normative)  
**Maintained by:** SCU Foundation

This Privacy Policy describes how the SCU Foundation handles logs, cookies, technical information, and third‑party services across its public websites.

The SCU Foundation operates with a strict minimal‑data philosophy.  
We do not collect personal information, do not track users across websites, and do not sell or share data with third parties.

---

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- the public website at **scu.foundation**  
- all subpaths under this domain  
- institutional pages  
- the HTML edition of the **SCU Whitepaper v1.8** and future editions  

This policy **does not apply** to external sites linked from this domain, including:

- **latticeprotocol.org**  
- **lattice.institute**  
- **p-sc.cc**  

External sites maintain their own privacy policies.

---

## 2. Minimal Data Collection

The SCU Foundation website is designed to operate with **minimal data collection**.

- Pages are served as static content.  
- No registration or account creation is required.  
- No personal information is requested or stored.  
- No behavioural analytics or marketing tools are used.

The Foundation does not build user profiles, behavioural datasets, or advertising identifiers.

---

## 3. Server Logs

Standard technical logs may be generated by the hosting environment.  
These logs can include:

- IP address  
- browser type  
- referring page  
- timestamps  
- basic request metadata  

These logs are used **solely** for:

- operational reliability  
- security monitoring  
- abuse prevention  
- diagnostics  

They are **not** used for marketing, tracking, or behavioural analysis.

---

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

The SCU Foundation does **not** use:

- analytics trackers  
- advertising pixels  
- behavioural profiling tools  
- third‑party marketing scripts  
- cross‑site tracking technologies  

If cookies are set, they are limited to **essential technical functions**, such as:

- load balancing  
- security  
- session integrity (non‑identifying)

No tracking cookies are used.

---

## 5. Third‑Party Services

Some assets may be served from third‑party providers, such as:

- fonts  
- embedded media  
- CDN‑hosted static files  

These services may receive basic technical information from your browser when content is requested.

Dependencies are kept minimal and are chosen for:

- stability  
- privacy  
- long‑term accessibility  

The SCU Foundation does not share additional data with these providers.

---

## 6. External Links

The SCU Foundation website may link to external resources or institutional partners, including:

- **Lattice Protocol**  
- **Lattice Research Institute**  
- **Post‑Silicon Collective**  

This Privacy Policy does **not** apply to those external sites.  
Users should review the privacy information provided by any third‑party service they choose to visit.
