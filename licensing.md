# Licensing  
**SCU Foundation — Legal & Compliance**  
**Status:** Canonical (Pre‑Normative)  
**Maintained by:** SCU Foundation

This document defines the licensing terms for SCU Foundation governance documents, standards, publications, and website content.  
The SCU Foundation is committed to open access, public‑good stewardship, and long‑term institutional neutrality.

Unless otherwise stated, materials published by the SCU Foundation are made available under open licenses that permit sharing, reuse, and citation with attribution.

---

## 1. Governance Documents

Governance‑root documents—including the Governance Charter, Global Body Summary, Standards Registry, Versioning Governance, Safety Governance, and related materials—are licensed under:

**Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)**  
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

You are free to:

- share — copy and redistribute the material  
- adapt — remix, transform, and build upon the material  

Under the following conditions:

- attribution — credit the SCU Foundation and indicate if changes were made  
- no implication of endorsement  

These documents are intended to support transparency, institutional clarity, and public‑sector adoption.

## 2. Standards and Specifications

Technical standards and specifications—including the Lattice Protocol, Lattice Web, Lattice Meta, Lattice Identity Token, and Trust Stack—are licensed under:

**Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)**

This ensures that:

- standards remain open and globally accessible  
- implementers may adopt, extend, or reference the specifications  
- derivative works do not imply endorsement or certification  

Certification requirements, where applicable, are defined separately in the governance corpus.

## 3. Publications

SCU Foundation publications—including the SCU Whitepaper v1.7, v1.8, and future editions—are licensed under:

**Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)**

## 5. Logos, Identity Marks, and Branding

The SCU Foundation’s logos, identity marks, and branding elements are **not** covered by CC BY 4.0.

They may **not** be used without written permission.

Prohibited uses include:

- implying endorsement  
- implying affiliation  
- using marks in derivative branding  
- using marks in commercial contexts  

Requests for permission may be directed to **contact@scu.foundation**.
